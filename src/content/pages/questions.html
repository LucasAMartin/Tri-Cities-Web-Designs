Frequently Asked Questions

We've put together some frequently asked questions to help you better understand our services. If you have any additional questions, don’t hesitate to contact us for more information.

  • The $150/mo cost is for a standard 5 page informational site. This includes all hosting/maintenance costs. If you need additional pages, they are available for $100 each. We also offer additional functionality such as booking and appointment scheduling for an additional fee of $200. These fees are one-time and due as a down payment before development begins.

  • If you decide to cancel your plan early, you will retain ownership of the domain and the written content on the page. However, the website code remains with us. Depending on the terms of your contract, you may be liable to pay the remainder of the months for the contract length. After the contract period is over, it becomes a month-to-month agreement and you can cancel anytime with no fees. The same code ownership rules apply in this case as well.

  • If you purchase lump sum, you have the full rights to the code and everything in it. With our monthly subscription plan, you have the rights to the domain and written content, but we retain the rights to the source code.

  • If you purchase a lump sum website, we offer 3 months of free hosting and support. Afterwards, with the $25/mo maintenance package you have the same services as the monthly plan. This includes unlimited edits, hosting, and maintenance for the length of the subscription. Otherwise, you will have to handle hosting manually.

  • Once the details are confirmed, we will have a first design mockup created for you within three (3) business days. Once you approve of a design you like, it may take up to seven (7) business days to complete development. We always make sure to keep you up to date with the latest progress as we develop your website.
