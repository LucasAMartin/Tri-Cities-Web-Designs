About Tri-Cities Web Designs

Bringing the Big Businesss treatment to Everybody

My name is Lucas, I am the founder and developer of Tri-Cities Web Designs. I started my journey in programming after taking my first few college programming classes. Three years later, I am now a senior at CWU about to graduate with my BS in Computer Science, developing websites for a living, using the skills and knowledge I gained through my studies and hands-on experience.

I quickly realized that many small businesses struggle to afford the high upfront costs of a professionally designed website. Even those who can afford it often end up with poorly designed websites that are outdated and unappealing. This is where I saw an opportunity to make a difference. I adjusted my business model to better meet the needs of small businesses by offering a $0 down and $150 per month payment plan. This makes it more manageable for small businesses to afford a high-quality website without breaking the bank.
